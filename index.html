Between epic and epideictic: direct speech in Claudian’s political poems through a digital lens

Valéry Berlincourt

This website provides HTML interactive versions of the figures contained in the following chapter:
Berlincourt, V. (2025), “Between epic and epideictic: direct speech in Claudian’s political poems through a digital lens”, in: C. Forstall, B. Verhelst (eds.), Multidisciplinary approaches to the representation of direct speech in Greek and Latin epic, Leiden–Boston.

Primary source of the data: DICES database (https://www.dices.uni-rostock.de), with minimal adjustments. Further data enrichment and calculations are my own; not-speech lines inserted within speeches are here discarded. The figures were programmed in Python with the Plotly open source library (https://plotly.com/python).
